We regularly publish these regulatory reports to demonstrate our ongoing efforts in content moderation, user and minor protection, and the mitigation of systemic risks, thereby ensuring a safe and compliant marketplace in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2022/2065 (the Digital Services Act, or "DSA") and other applicable laws.

Independent Audit Report under the Digital Services Act

The Independent Audit Report is published pursuant to Article 42(4)(c) of Regulation (EU) 2022/2065 (the Digital Services Act, or "DSA"). It presents the results of the independent audit conducted under Article 37 of the DSA, following Temu's designation by the European Commission as a Very Large Online Platform ("VLOP") on 31 May 2024.

Reporting Period:

3 Oct 2024 - 30 Jun 2025

Audit Implementation Report under the Digital Services Act

The Audit Implementation Report is published pursuant to Article 42(4)(d) of Regulation (EU) 2022/2065 (the Digital Services Act, or "DSA"). It outlines Temu's response to, and where relevant, the implementation plans for the findings and recommendations identified in the independent audit conducted under Article 37 of the DSA.

Reporting Period:

3 Oct 2024 - 30 Jun 2025

Risk Assessment and Mitigation Report under the Digital Services Act

This Risk Assessment and Mitigation Report is published pursuant to Article 42(4)(a) and (b) of Regulation (EU) 2022/2065 (the Digital Services Act, or "DSA"). It presents the findings of our identification, analysis, and assessments of systemic risks pursuant to Article 34 of the DSA, alongside specific measures implemented to mitigate these risks pursuant to Article 35 of the DSA.

Reporting Period:

1 Jul 2024 - 30 Jun 2025

Transparency Reports under the Digital Services Act

The Transparency Reports are published pursuant to Articles 15, 24 and 42 of Regulation (EU) 2022/2065 (the Digital Services Act, or "DSA"). These reports provide a comprehensive overview of Temu's content moderation efforts, ensuring the continued safety and compliance of the platform for all users and business partners within the European Union.

Reporting Period:
